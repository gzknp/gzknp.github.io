Word Factory

Word Factory provides a convenient way to learn words in English -by categories!-. Our approach to learning is based on the idea that we -mostly- happen to remember the words that are associated with categories we're closely interested in. So, by using Word Factory, you'll find a chance to learn words that are related to your interests.

By using Word Factory, you'll:

  • Learn new words,
  • Listen newly-learned words,
  • Expand your vocabulary,
  • Test your skills,
  • Keep your words safe with a cloud backup!
